Description

A classic burger recipe with all the fixings. Perfect for a summer cookout or a quick and easy meal.

Ingredients

  • Hamburger Meat
  • Buns of your choosing
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Any other Topic of your choosing

Steps

  1. Form the hamburger meat into patties
  2. Cook the patties to your liking
  3. Toast the buns
  4. Assemble the burger with your chosen toppings
  5. Enjoy!
